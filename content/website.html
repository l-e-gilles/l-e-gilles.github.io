Design Research
After some intial research looking at other people's portfolio websites, I created a moodboard with the design elements I wanted to incorporate into my website.
Moodboard
1. Was drawn to the project tiles with a very simple image of the project

2. Liked how the different factors in a project were clearly outlined and concise

3. I liked this home page with the art graphic

4. Wanted to incorporate gradients somehow

5. Liked the UI with the white title

6. Create some kind of tile or banner with the UI

Initial Sketch

I started organizing my ideas with a hand drawn wireframe, thinking about what information and functionality I wanted to include and the flow of navigation.

I also listed some keyphrases to keep in mind when designing.

Designing
From there, I ceated a final low-fidelity wireframe in figma based on my initial sketch.

Initially I planned for the resume page to include graphics, but instead made a second version. I opted for something that would be easier to manage and update.

I chose not to create a high-fidelity wireframe since I had a good idea of what I wanted the website to look like and start coding than taking more time to finalize
